Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

Welcome to Spark ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Pet name and customization preferences
  • Photos: Images you choose to upload to your shared photo album
  • Usage Data: Game scores, activity history, and app interactions
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our app services
  • Enable features like shared photo albums and multiplayer games
  • Sync data between you and your partner
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Send important service notifications

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication
  • Row-level security policies to protect your data

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is shared only with:

  • Your Partner: Data you create together (photos, game scores, pet status) is shared between paired accounts
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services (Supabase for data storage, Google AdMob for advertising)

5. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can manage ad preferences through your device settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the app settings. When you delete your account:

  • All your personal data will be permanently removed
  • Your shared content (photos, game history) will be deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

To delete your account, visit: Account Deletion Page

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
